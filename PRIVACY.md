# Privacy Policy for Green Timer & Stopwatch

This extension respects your privacy. 

### Data Collection
- **No Personal Data:** This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to external servers.
- **Local/Sync Storage:** All data (timer settings, problem logs, and history) is stored locally on your device or synced through your browser's official synchronization service (Google Account or Firefox Account).

### Data Usage
- The data is used solely to provide the extension's core functionality: tracking problem-solving time and maintaining a history of your activities.

### Third Parties
- No data is shared with third parties.

### Contact
If you have any questions, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/nxorg/green-timer
