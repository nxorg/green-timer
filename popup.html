Be the best time tracker and habit builder to help you become a coding master.

Basic features:

  • ✅ Track time for multiple LeetCode problems
  • ✅ Analyze your habits and generate visual reports
  • ✅ Automatically detected problem details

Advanced features:

  • ✅ Matrix-themed dark mode animation
  • ✅ Sync data with Google and Firefox accounts
  • ✅ Export your history to CSV

How to use:

  • 1. Pin the green icon
  • 2. Browse LeetCode problems as usual
  • 3. Click the icon to detect and track data
PROMISE: All data is stored in your browser's Local/Sync storage and never sent to 3rd party servers.

Available in multiple browsers:

  • ✅ Chrome
  • ✅ Firefox
  • ✅ Edge
